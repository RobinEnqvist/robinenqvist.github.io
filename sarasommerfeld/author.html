Playwright · Screenwriter · Novelist

Author

Sara Sommerfeld creates across forms — as playwright, screenwriter and novelist. Her work moves between theatre, film and literature, often exploring themes of identity, belonging and inheritance.

She is a graduate of Alma Manusutbildning and has completed advanced writing programmes at Stockholm University of the Arts and SVT. Her debut novel sold 20,000 copies in its first year.

Books

Debut Novel · 2025

Is Everything I Learned About Love a Lie?

Är allt jag lärt mig om kärlek en lögn?

An emotional autobiographical novel written with self-distance and a large measure of humour. Sara Sommerfeld unabashedly faces her self-hatred, doubts and fragile self-esteem — and travels to Bali to find herself. With Elizabeth Gilbert's Eat, Pray, Love as a companion, it becomes both a dramatic and life-changing journey. A novel about traveling far away to find home, about the search for one's true self and the killing of one's inner perfectionist.
