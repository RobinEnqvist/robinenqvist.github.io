Juna • Erinion • Industrial Batteries • Lots

Juna

Juna is one of the latest recruits to the group. With a mind like a blade — razor-sharp and intelligent — she runs on pure, sweet electricity.

And she also makes it possible for everyone to share that electricity; having created a smart, easy to use, electric truck as-a-Service system. What drives her: transitioning to zero-emission road logistics.

Erinion

Erinion enables charging infrastructure for heavy duty road transport. That means he's always at your side, wherever you are on your electrification journey.

Don't misjudge him for his charming, feisty look — Erinion is the calmest, most levelheaded person of the group. He stays peaceful, by your side; no matter the circumstances and where you're headed.

Industrial Batteries

Their name is pretty straight to the point. They are: battery systems, simplifying off-road electrification.

An energetic twin pair; never staying still or quiet. Although they're constantly bouncing off the walls — they actually have an important goal. To de-risk electrification through predictable, reliable performance.

Lots

Ever since her birth, Lots has known what her purpose in life is: to dismantle the Emissions Empire. This has led to her already having created the world's largest net-zero logistics ecosystem.

The youngest of them all, but also the brains of the group. She spends the days crouched by her computer, transitioning complex data into actionable insights.
