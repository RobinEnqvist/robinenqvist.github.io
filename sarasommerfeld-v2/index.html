Sara Sommerfeld is an award-winning multilingual Swedish actress with more than three decades of experience across film, television and theatre.

She rose to prominence opposite Alexander Skarsgård in the feature film Wings of Glass and has since appeared in over 50 leading roles across film, television and theatre. Her work has earned her the Best Actress Award at the Nordic Film Festival in Haugesund and a Guldbagge nomination for Best Actress.

Alongside her acting career, Sara writes and directs her own work, including the critically acclaimed solo performance Show Bizniz, presented on the Main Stage of Sweden's Royal Dramatic Theatre and later broadcast on Swedish national television.

Writer · Director · Playwright

Creator

Playwright Director Author

Alongside her acting career, Sara Sommerfeld creates character-driven stories as a writer, director and playwright.

Her work moves between theatre, film and literature, often exploring themes of identity, belonging and inheritance. Sara is educated in directing and screenwriting at Stockholm University of Dramatic Arts, and is a graduate of the acclaimed screenwriting school Alma Manusutbildning.

Playwright debut · 2023

Show Bizniz

Dramaten Main Stage · Sweden Tour · SVT Broadcast 2025

In 2023, Sara made her playwriting debut with the critically acclaimed solo performance Show Bizniz on the Main Stage of Sweden's Royal Dramatic Theatre. The production later toured Sweden and was broadcast by SVT.

As Author

With the same unflinching honesty she brings to the stage, Sara Sommerfeld writes autobiographical novels that blend self-inquiry with warmth and humor.

Debut Novel · 2024

Is Everything I Learned About Love a Lie?

An emotional autobiographical novel about traveling far away to find home — written with self-distance and a large measure of humor. With Elizabeth Gilbert's Eat, Pray, Love as a companion, it becomes a dramatic and life-changing journey.
