Why each section of this prompt matters
Anomaly Detection Rules
Hardcoded thresholds mean the agent catches performance drops before your morning review — not after you've burned through budget.
Insight Structure (PAS / SOAR)
Every observation ends with a concrete action. The agent never reports a problem without recommending a fix.
Feedback Routing Rules
Closes the loop. Winning signals flow back to Creative and Research automatically — no manual briefing needed.
Human Approval Gates
Defines exactly where AI autonomy ends and human judgment begins — so nothing big happens without your sign-off.
Cadence Discipline
Daily, weekly, and monthly rhythms ensure insight flows at the right altitude — operational vs strategic — without noise.
Attribution Context
The agent knows which attribution model to trust — so it never misreads Meta ROAS vs true blended performance.
